# 💎 Kosimila Luxury E-commerce: Features Catalog

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the features and technical capabilities implemented in the Kosimila premium makhana store.

---

## 1. 🛍️ Premium Shopping Experience

### Luxury Product Catalog
*   **Aesthetic Listing**: High-contrast, airy UI with rounded "card-premium" designs.
*   **Intelligent Filtering**: Top-pill category navigation for instant discovery.
*   **Advanced Sorting**: Sort by Newest, Price (High/Low), and Top Rated.
*   **Interactive Product Cards**: Hover-triggered "Quick View" overlays, dynamic discount badges (e.g., "15% OFF"), and "Best Seller" tags.
*   **Live Search engine**: Horizontal search with real-time suggestions including product images, prices, and "Recently Viewed" history.

### High-Conversion Product Pages
*   **Dynamic Variants**: Support for multiple weights (500g, 1kg, 2kg) with automatic price and stock updates.
*   **Smart Inventory**: "Out of Stock" variants are automatically disabled to prevent checkout friction.
*   **Sticky "Quick Buy" Bar**: A mobile-optimized bar that appears as users scroll, keeping the "Add to Cart" action always accessible.
*   **Recently Viewed Section**: LocalStorage-powered "Continue Browsing" section to increase return visits.
*   **Bulk Inquiry System**: Dedicated "Wholesale Inquiry" button that opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled professional message for 10kg+ orders.

### Smart Cart & Checkout
*   **Shipping Calculator**: Dynamic progress bar in the cart that nudges users (e.g., "Add 500g more for FREE delivery").
*   **Gift-Ready Checkout**: Toggleable "Premium Gift Option" allowing users to add a personalized printed note to their order.
*   **Address Management**: Save multiple delivery addresses for one-click future checkouts.
*   **Serviceability Check**: Real-time pincode verification to ensure delivery is available before payment.
*   **COD Integration**: Streamlined Cash on Delivery flow for the Indian market.

---

## 2. 👩‍🍳 Content & Engagement: The Recipe Club

### Dynamic Culinary Section
*   **CMS-Managed Recipes**: A dedicated section for "Makhana Creations" where the client can post healthy recipes.
*   **Search & Filter**: Find recipes by category (Savory, Sweet, Main Course) or specific ingredients.
*   **Gourmet Detail Pages**: Beautifully formatted pages with cooking time, calorie counts, difficulty levels, and "Chef's Tips."
*   **Ingredient Linking**: Direct links from recipes back to the product shop to drive immediate sales.

---

## 3. 👤 Customer Member Dashboard

### Premium Member Area
*   **Luxury Overview**: Dashboard showing recent orders, loyalty status (Signature/Gold/Platinum), and account stats.
*   **Order Journey Tracker**: A visual horizontal timeline tracking orders from *Placed* to *Confirmed* to *Delivered*.
*   **Interactive History**: Re-order capabilities and detailed order summaries including gift notes.
*   **Address Book**: Manage, edit, and set default shipping locations with ease.
*   **Concierge Support**: One-click access to WhatsApp and Email support from within the tracking page.

---

## 4. ⚙️ Admin & Backend Control

### Full Management Suite
*   **Product Manager**: Create/Edit products with rich descriptions, multiple images, and variants.
*   **Recipe CMS**: A full-featured editor for the client to add new culinary content, slugs, and nutritional data.
*   **Order Management**: Track and update order statuses (Processing, Shipped, etc.) which reflects instantly on the user's tracker.
*   **Category & Banner Control**: Manage site-wide navigation and marketing announcements.

---

## 5. 🛠️ Technical Excellence
*   **SEO Optimized**: Dynamic OpenGraph meta tags for beautiful social media sharing (WhatsApp/Instagram).
*   **Mobile-First Design**: Every feature is optimized for high-end mobile devices.
*   **Luxury Performance**: Fast-loading pages with Next.js App Router and Prisma ORM.
*   **Security**: JWT-based authentication for secure user and admin access.
